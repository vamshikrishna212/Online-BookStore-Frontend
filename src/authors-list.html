Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

APJ Abdul Kalam, known as the "Missile Man of India," was a renowned aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a pivotal role in India's civilian space and military missile development efforts. A beloved leader and visionary, he inspired millions through his teachings on education, innovation, and national development.

Sri Sri

Sri Sri, or Srirangam Srinivasa Rao, was a revolutionary Telugu poet and lyricist known for his progressive and socially conscious poetry. He played a significant role in bringing modernism to Telugu literature, particularly through his seminal work "Maha Prasthanam," which reflects themes of social justice, inequality, and human struggle. Sri Sri's writing resonated with the common people, making him a beloved literary figure and an influential voice in Telugu literature.

Sirivennela Sitarama Sastry

Sirivennela Sitarama Sastry, also known as Chembolu Sitarama Sastry, was a legendary Telugu lyricist renowned for his profound and poetic lyrics in Telugu cinema. He gained immense popularity with his work in the 1986 film "Sirivennela," which earned him the moniker "Sirivennela" and solidified his place in Telugu literary and film history. His lyrics are celebrated for their deep philosophical insights, cultural richness, and emotional depth. Over his illustrious career, he penned thousands of songs that continue to resonate with audiences, making him one of the most respected and beloved lyricists in Telugu cinema..
