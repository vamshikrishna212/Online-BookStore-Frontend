Bloomsbury India

Bloomsbury India is the Indian subsidiary of Bloomsbury Publishing, a globally recognized independent publishing house based in the UK. Established in India in 2012, Bloomsbury India publishes a wide range of genres, including fiction, non-fiction, academic, and children's books.

Rupa Publications

Rupa Publications is one of India's oldest and most respected publishing houses, established in 1936. It is known for its wide range of publications, including fiction, non-fiction, biographies, memoirs, and self-help books. Rupa Publications has played a pivotal role in shaping the Indian literary landscape, publishing works by some of the country's most celebrated authors, as well as introducing new and emerging voices.
